What's In / What's Out

What's in. What's out.

A plain-English summary of what the contract covers, what is quoted as an add-on, and what falls outside Walldot's scope so you can plan for it separately.

Included

  • All civil structural work as per approved drawings
  • Brick and block masonry, plastering, waterproofing
  • Internal and external painting (one coat primer + two finish)
  • Standard electrical wiring + fixtures (per BoQ)
  • Standard plumbing rough-in + sanitary fixtures (per BoQ)
  • Flooring as per the approved selection
  • Standard kitchen counter + sink (per scope card)
  • Doors, windows, grill work as quoted
  • One coat of weatherproof exterior finish
  • Site supervision and quality checks

Add-ons quoted

  • Premium tile / flooring upgrades (variance shown in master table)
  • Modular kitchen and wardrobes (per customization line)
  • False ceiling and decorative lighting
  • Elevation customizations (page 15)
  • Designer paint finishes / textured walls
  • Smart-home wiring / automation pre-rough-in
  • Premium bathroom fittings as per option selected
  • Approved structural extensions / additions

Not covered

  • Interior furniture, soft furnishings, and curtains
  • Loose appliances (TV, fridge, washing machine, etc.)
  • Landscaping, lawn, and exterior planters
  • Compound wall and gate (unless quoted separately)
  • Government / society approval fees and taxes outside GST
  • Borewell, water tank above standard size
  • Solar panels, inverter, generator
  • Architect / consultant fees if engaged separately

Quality & Escalation

Quality protocol & escalation.

We run three quality gates per stage and provide three escalation levels for any issue you raise. Site visit cadence is fixed and is recorded as part of each milestone hand-over.

Pre-check

Materials, drawings and site conditions verified before the stage kicks off. Any non-conformity is documented and resolved before work starts.

Interim check

Workmanship measured against the spec at 25 / 50 / 75% completion. Photographic evidence stored against the milestone in the customer app.

Final check

Stage-end walk-through with the customer; deficiencies are listed in the milestone snag report and closed out before the next stage starts.
